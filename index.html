No elaborate productivity system

Every session is a job.

Open Focus or Ketchup, accept one 25-minute focus job and keep your word. No projects to configure, no planning ritual and no room for excuses.

01

Accept the job

One tap starts a clean 25-minute focus session. Choose what matters and begin immediately.

02

Keep your word

The Family sends timely reminders so you do not forget what you promised. Start, pause, finish or walk away. The Don will have an opinion.

03

Earn respect

Completed jobs build your streak and focused time. Your record speaks for itself.
