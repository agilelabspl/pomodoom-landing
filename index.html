Start fast. Stay focused. Survive the tomato.

No projects to configure and no task to type in. Open Pomodoom, hit Start and give one thing your full attention.

More than a countdown

Your timer has opinions.

Killer Tomato reacts to starting, pausing, finishing and giving up. Pomodoom is designed to feel like a character keeping you company, not another sterile utility.

“Focus time. Try not to embarrass us both.”
“Three minutes left. Finish what you started.”
“Session complete. Acceptable.”
